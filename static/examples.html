API Examples


Search by slang


Submit slang or abbreviated form of a word (like 'smillleee') to find words associated with it

http://word-redsquirrel.dotcloud.com/wordslang?token=smillleee&check=slang&output=word


Submit slang to find the phonetic format of the word associated with it

http://word-redsquirrel.dotcloud.com/wordslang?token=smillleee&check=slang&output=pho


Check whether the slang term exists in the db

http://word-redsquirrel.dotcloud.com/wordslang?token=smillleee&check=slang&output=exists


Search by word


Submit a word and get back it's slang versions

http://word-redsquirrel.dotcloud.com/wordslang?token=smile&check=word&output=slang


Submit a word and get back its phonetic representation

http://word-redsquirrel.dotcloud.com/wordslang?token=smile&check=word&output=pho


Check whether the slang term exists in the db

http://word-redsquirrel.dotcloud.com/wordslang?token=smile&check=word&output=exists


Search by phonetic


Submit the phonetic representation and get back the corresponding word(s)

http://word-redsquirrel.dotcloud.com/wordslang?token=sml&check=pho&output=word


Check whether a phonetic representation exists in the db

http://word-redsquirrel.dotcloud.com/wordslang?token=sml&check=pho&output=exists


Emoticon-based Queries


Get words that correpond to a submitted emoticon

http://word-redsquirrel.dotcloud.com/ws?token=:-)&check=emo&output=word


Other cool stuff


Check all fields for a token and return the word

http://word-redsquirrel.dotcloud.com/wordslang?token=smile&check=all&output=word


As above, but return all fields

http://word-redsquirrel.dotcloud.com/wordslang?token=smile&check=all&output=all


Either end of token wildcard search

http://word-redsquirrel.dotcloud.com/wordslang?token=ile&check=slang&output=word&regex=True


Dump out a unique list of all phonetics

http://word-redsquirrel.dotcloud.com/distinctdump?check=pho


Dump out a unique list of all words

http://word-redsquirrel.dotcloud.com/distinctdump?check=word


Dump out a unique list of all words begining with the letter a (as a way of reducing the query)

http://word-redsquirrel.dotcloud.com/distinctdump?check=word&output=a


Dump out a unique list of all words (works for pho too - just change check=) of between 2 and 4 characters long

http://word-redsquirrel.dotcloud.com/distinctdump?check=word&wordlength=3&edits=1
